Interesting Places in Canterbury

Christ Church Cathedral

A prominent Anglican cathedral, rebuilt after significant damage.

Canterbury Museum

A leading museum in Canterbury, reflecting the city's changing cultural landscape.

Canterbury Provincial Council Buildings

A Victorian architectural masterpiece.

Christ's College

A historic school with several buildings listed as Category 1 historic places.

The Arts Centre

A significant collection of heritage buildings, now a thriving arts and culture hub.

Ferrymead Heritage Park

Recreates early European settlement, showcasing lives of Christchurch's early residents.

Riccarton House & Bush

A historic homestead with gardens and bush, offering a glimpse into the past.

Canterbury Earthquake National Memorial

A place of remembrance for the 2010 and 2011 earthquakes.

Kate Sheppard House

A Category 1 historic place, dedicated to the suffragette Kate Sheppard.
